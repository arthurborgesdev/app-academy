Lemon Pie

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Description

A recipe to make the most delicious Lemon Pie!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Egg whites
  • Sweet Condensed Milk
  • Butter
  • Pie Crust

Directions

  1. Pre heat the oven
  2. Put the milk, egg whites, sweet condensed milk, butter and pie crust in a saucepan
  3. Stir them
  4. Bake
  5. Enjoy!
